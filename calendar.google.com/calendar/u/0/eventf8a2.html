Course Offering Fair

The MV Course Offering Fair (formerly the MV Elective Fair) will be on Thursday, February 27th at lunch (12:35 - 1:10) in the Amphitheater.  I just sent you all a calendar invite with the date and time.  Students will be getting their course offering forms in English classes on February 26th and 27th.  They will be turning in their course offering forms during the week of March 2nd.

 

Anyone may participate in this event, not just elective teachers.  Our courses are now competing against online classes so this is our opportunity to advertise what makes our courses different than their online equivalents.  Students, especially juniors and seniors, have many choices for English, Science, etc.  This is a way for you to explain, showcase, and recruit for your course(s).  Feel free to bring visual aids and handouts, as well as current students to talk about how much they love your class.
