RACC College Fair - Amphitheatre

Out-of-State College Fair (In-Person @ MV!) | Hosted by RACC

Thursday, September 23rd from 5:00-7:00pm

Location: Amphitheater @ MV

RACC (Regional Admission Counselors of California) are coming to MV in-person! We will have a number of out-of-state colleges to talk to our students about the admission process and what makes their college unique. Parents and students are encouraged to participate. Please note that this event is open to all four SRVUSD high schools so I would encourage you to come as early as possible. Save the Date! Please fill out the form here. 

RACC Colleges who will be visiting MV: Marist College, TCU, LSU, University of Colorado @ Colorado Springs, Colorado State University, Clarkson University, Northern Arizona University, University of Arizona, Massachusetts College of Pharmacy & Health Sciences, Oregon State University, Michigan State University, University of Pittsburgh, University of Idaho. Contact Sarah Wells with questions. swells@srvusd.net
