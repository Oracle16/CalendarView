PSAT for 10/11 Graders


The PSAT is a preliminary test taken by students to help them practice for the SAT. This test is different than the ACT/SAT Training offered by Academic Boosters during off school hours. For grade 11 students, the PSAT also serves as the National Merit Scholarship Qualifying Test. For more information on the PSAT, please go to the College Board website on PSAT.


The District is committed to offering the PSAT to all 10th and 11th grade students regardless of ability to pay. However, there is a cost associated with taking the test, and Monte Vista is asking for a donation to help cover the cost. Please go to Future Fund to make a donation. Thanks so much.
